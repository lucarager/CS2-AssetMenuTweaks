Mod created in collaboration with CityPlannerPlays for the Dutch City Challenge.

Check out the official CityPlannerPlays video https://youtu.be/9q2BXEPoHao

**Welcome to the Dutch City Challenge!**

To participate, you must complete the challenge using ONLY the official playset:
https://mods.paradoxplaza.com/playsets/cities_skylines_2/11048476

You can attempt the challenge as many times as you’d like, but please only submit your single best run.
Join the CPP Discord to submit your entry! https://discord.com/invite/cityplannerplays

**Challenge Rules**
- 1-hour only.  The mod has a built-in timer that stops counting after 60 minutes.
- Game Speed: Simulating faster than the vanilla maximum is NOT allowed. Do __not__ enable 8x speed via dev mod.
- Only use the official playset.  No other mods are allowed
- All paid content is allowed.

**Submission Requirements**
When submitting, please provide:
- A link to your save file. (used to verify no other mods were used)
- A screenshot that clearly shows both your total number of bike trips and the in-game date.

**Prize and Participation Restrictions**
Prizes are as follows:
- 1st place: $100 Steam or CPP Store Gift Card
- 2nd Place: $50 Steam or CPP Store Gift Card
- 3rd Place: $25 Steam or CPP Store Gift Card

Steam Prizes will be sent via Steam digital gifts.  CPP Store gifts will be sent as codes for specific items of your choosing.

Due to Steam platform restrictions, prizes unfortunately cannot be sent to certain regions—including countries with highly fluctuating economies, war, or other local restrictions.

Ineligible countries for steam gifts include, but are not limited to: Argentina, China, India, Turkey, and Russia.  CPP store gifts subject to normal store shipping restrictions.
